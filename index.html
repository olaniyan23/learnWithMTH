what we stand for

our vision

Our vision is to have a community where information technology is perceived as accessible to all women.By providing opportunities for women to be incoperated in the industry and havin a smooth transition regardless of previous background.

We intend to train over 5000 women in information Technology and ensure the transitioning to employment for more than 70% of the women.

our mission

Our mission is to make the field of Infomation Technology accessible to all women from all works of life. We are pursuing inclusion for women regardless of age financial status, income bracket and background.

Our mission is to empower women to pursue and live purpose-driven lives by making the field of Information Technology accessible to all women.

empowerher©

Empowerher community is a tech based community focused on training and promoting women in the field of Information Technology. We aim to spraed more awareness for opportunity in Information technology and incoperate as many women as possible.
